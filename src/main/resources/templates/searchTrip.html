Visit Soroca`s Fortress

Soroca Fortress is a historic fort in the Republic of Moldova, in the modern-day city of Soroca.

Visit Curchi`s Monastery

Curchi`s Monastery is a monastery located in the Republic of Moldova, being one of the most significat architectural monuments in the Republic of Moldova.

Purcari is a Moldovan wine producer from Purcari Wine Region, located in Ștefan Vodă District, Moldova. Through the years Vinaria Purcari won over 100 international medals.

Visit Old Orhei

Old Orhei is a historical and archaeological complex, located in Trebujeni, at 60 kilometres (37 mi) north-east of Chişinău, on the Răut River, in Moldova.
